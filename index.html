Vision

A new global value system where beneficial behavior compounds. AI makes impact measurable. Blockchain makes it ownable.

Health Span

Reward actions that strengthen long-term physical & mental resilience—habits that extend quality of life.

Wealth Growth

Earth Benefit

Align incentives with sustainability through measurable reductions in waste and positive ecological behavior.

How it Works

Four layers: real-world capture → AI verification → scoring → minting. No raw video on-chain.

1

AI Glasses (Input Layer)

Opt-in, first-person sensing to understand actions in context (movement, work, learning, environmental choices).

2

Impact Intelligence (AI Layer)

Models classify “impact primitives” and create Proof of Beneficial Action—a privacy-safe impact signal.

3

Human Value Score (Scoring)

Time-weighted, decay-aware scoring that reduces gaming and rewards consistent contribution over time.

4

On-Chain Minting (Value Layer)

Verified impact is recorded and rewarded on-chain (reputation + reward tokens, or a hybrid model).

Ethics & Safeguards

This idea only works if it is privacy-first, user-sovereign, and auditable.

Privacy & trust principles
  • Opt-in only with clear consent and controls.
  • Local processing where possible; minimize data leaving the device.
  • No raw video on-chain; only hashed / abstracted impact proofs.
  • Transparent scoring + independent audits of models and contracts.

On-chain Value

120LAB can support multiple token architectures depending on regulation and product direction.

Reputation (SBT)

Non-transferable identity/reputation for impact history. Best for trust, credentials, and access.

Rewards (Token)

Transferable token minted from verified positive impact. Best for incentives and market dynamics.

Hybrid Model

Reputation anchors the human identity; rewards provide liquidity—while reducing farming and fraud.
